Top 3 Pole Vaulting Facts

  1. First Olympics
    • 1896 Summer olympics in Athens, Greece
    • William Hoyt of the United States took Gold
    • 3.30 meters
  2. World Record
    • Mondo Duplantis
    • 6.27 meters (just over 20 feet)
    • Broken the record 11 times
  3. The Stick
    • Made of metal and fiberglass
    • Adopted in the 1950s after using sticks of ash, steel, and bamboo
